Our Love Story

First Meet

We met in church. He was coming to my church for school of discipleship after service every Sunday. I was that choir girl that was always around after service even though I wasn't in school of discipleship yet so we got talking sha.

First Date

He invited me to his sister's wedding anddd asked that we hang out the day after the wedding. So we went to the zoo..it was lovely. We walked around and looked at the animals, gisted and went to eat after. P.S He asked me out there too.

Proposal

That happened on the first date. After we were done seeing the animals, he asked that we sit and talk, We did and this fine man started telling me how beautiful I was, thanking me for being a part of his life. And that he'll love our relationship to shift from mentor-mentee to my girl-my guy. That we should be together for more years than the animals at the zoo had lived, the moment was so sweet, I said yes and that I'd like to go on this journey with him. 600years for us Baby!

Enagagement

This was one Sunday after service. He said he wanted us to go out, he wants to take me on a treat. We went with some of our friends. Everyone ordered food but I said I was full, I didn't want to eat, but just drink. But then he insisted he was going to order something for me and violaaa they came with a platter saying "will you marry me?" I laughed a lot. And said yes after he spoke, it was beautiful.
